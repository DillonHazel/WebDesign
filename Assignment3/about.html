Movie Overview

Johnny Dangerously is a classic comedy published December 21, 1984. It has amazing intellectual jokes followed by hysterical lapstick routines. The story follows Johnny as a poor kid living in New York who accidentally becomes a well respected criminal and night club owner. He must avoid the attention of his little brother and prosecuter. It's a barrel of laughs, please watch it.
