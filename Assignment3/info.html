Director

Johnny Dangerously was directed by Amy Heckerling. She also directed comedy films like Clueless, I Could Never Be Your Woman, and Fast Times at Ridgemont High.

Screen Writers

Johnny Dangerously had four screen Writers. Norman Steinberg, Bernie Kukoff, Harry Colomby, and Jeff Harris.
